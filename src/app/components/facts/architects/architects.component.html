Architects

Most profssional architects are worth the investment, they
are very good at designing individual client requirements;
personalised scope of works; a schedule of finishes and
detailed specifcations.
This is what we as professional builders need to allow us to
provide accurate pricing in advance which minimises changes
and surprises.
Architects are neither builders nor estimators.
They can only give you a guide using historical prices for
their designs. ONLY if the project is recent and very similar.
Trust your professional builder to provide accurate pricing
with backup data alternative selections if available.
