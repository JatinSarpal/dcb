Timing to consider

You will generally need to consult an Engineer.
this should be done very early on in consultation
with your builder and architect, but do not do
your final drawings until you have approval.
Councils take a long time to approve works (this
varies between councils) so plan accordingly and
expect this.
Sydney Water may hold up your approval as well
if there are assets belonging to then near your
proposed building works.
